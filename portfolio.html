Objectives and System Functionalities The motivation of the project is to simplify the home furnishing and shopping process to customers, by bringing customers and furniture companies together in a very well designed e-shopping system that will give the user the ability to furnish his home and visualize his dreams using a stunning graphics and ultra 3D models of real furniture. The main objectives are: • Solving the problems of loss of time, effort & money, travelling abroad, stock availability, and limitation of available items, by developing an e-commerce website to act as the meeting point between customers & furniture companies. • Solving the problems of furniture’s symmetry, sizes and fitting, by developing a designing application tools to help the user furnishing his home and see whether the furniture fit together or not, before buying
